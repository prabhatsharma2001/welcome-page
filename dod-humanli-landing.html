Numbers are computed, not generated
The language model does not produce financial numbers. All variance calculations, driver isolations, and KPI breakdowns are produced through deterministic computation logic before any explanation is generated.
⇌
Business rules are traceable
Every calculation applies defined business rules from the Knowledge Bank. Rules are versioned, attributed, and surfaced in the audit trail — not embedded invisibly in model weights.
⊕
Recommendations link to source evidence
Every recommendation is tied to the data, calculation, and rule that produced it. Leadership can trace the action back to the evidence before acting on it.
◬
Architecture designed for regulated environments
Built to support deployment in finance, power, manufacturing, and government contexts where decisions require full auditability and defensible source lineage.
